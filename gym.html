Gym

Our spacious gym features a wide range of state of the art gym equipment. The equipment we provide is suitable for all types of gym users from novices to experienced gym users. We pride ourselves in being able to cater for all gym users with a welcoming, stimulating and progressive environment.

Our personal trainers are always there to help you tailor your workout. We also hold tutor led classes in a wide range of exercises. For those who are new or just want to mix it up our fitness coaches provide ‘touch base’ sessions where you learn about new equipment and are encouraged to try something different in order to enhance your exercise program and help you vary your routine.

The Gym's changing rooms are equipped with showering facilities and a steam room. Our changing rooms contain both open changing and changing cubicles.

To book with us you can either use our messaging service or call 16431879196842.
