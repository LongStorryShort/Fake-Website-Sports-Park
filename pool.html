Swimming Pool

Enjoy swimming in our 50 metre pool or junior pool. You can enjoy a pleasant swim in our large pool or enjoy a day out with your family. Our helpful lifeguards are always by the pool to help you. We also can provide swimmer with some pool equipment if requested. The pool is kept very clean because of our filter and is clean at least once a day.

We also provide our customers with the chance to do life guard lessons. The course is subsidised by the sports centre itself and there is the possibility of employment upon completion.

We provide 3 different changing rooms for the swimming pool with not just gender specific changing but also a family changing facility.

To book with us you can either use our messaging service or call 16431879196842.
