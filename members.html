Memberships

We offer a wide range memberships that can be tailored to your needs and budget. We offer both full and partial memberships. Basic full memberships start from just £15.00 whilst the partial memberships start from just £5.00 with a £1.00 entry fee.

When you first sign up to a full membership you get 1 month free.

Full Memberships

A full membership to just one of our facilities costs only £15.00 a month. With this deal you choose the facility and also covers any classes you may take.

A full membership of multiple facilities cost only £25.00. With this deal you can choose which 2 of our facilities and have the same privileges as all other full members.

Our all-inclusive full membership costs just £45.00 and covers all of our facilities.

Partial Membership

A Partial membership is for just one facility and costs £5.00 plus every time you use the facility you pay £1.00.

For more information you can either use our messaging service or call 16431879196842.
