Sports Hall

In our spacious 4 court sports hall we host a wide range of activities. Along with the 4 court main hall we also provide 2 squash courts and an observation deck. These facilities can be used for a wide range of activities such as badminton or football; we also offer our customer the option to have children's birthday parties in the hall. The equipment for these activities can be provided by us or you can bring your own.
